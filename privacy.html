Privacy Policy

Last updated: 6 July 2026

InvoicePe is a free, browser-based invoice generator that lets freelancers and small businesses create GST-ready invoices without signing up. This Privacy Policy explains what information the tool handles and how your privacy is protected when you use it.

Data We Collect

We designed InvoicePe to be privacy-first. All the invoice information you enter — your business details, client details, line items, tax rates, and currency preference — is stored only in your own web browser using localStorage.

  • Your data never leaves your device. It is not transmitted to, processed by, or stored on any server or database that we control.
  • We do not require sign-up, login, or account creation, and we do not collect personal data on our servers.
  • Because the data lives in your browser, clearing your browser storage or using a different device or browser will remove or hide your saved details. You are always in full control of this information.
